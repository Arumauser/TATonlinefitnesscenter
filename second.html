Exercises To Do At Home


Lunge

Lunges use large lower-body muscles, including the glutes, hamstrings, and quadriceps.

Push-up

Building upper body strength, reducing the risk of cardiac events, and improving body composition.

Pike Push-up

A pike push-up is a bodyweight exercise that targets the shoulders, triceps, and upper back muscles.

Squat

A strength exercise in which the trainee lowers their hips from a standing position and then stands back up.

Yoga

Yoga is a practice that connects the body, breath, and mind. It uses physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to improve overall health.

Pull ups

Pullups are a popular and effective exercise for strengthening your upper back and biceps.

Planks

The plank is an isometric core strength exercise that involves maintaining a position similar to a push-up for the maximum possible time.

Knee Planks

Lie face down on the ground with your legs together and your arms at your sides. Position your hands beneath your shoulders.

Overhead dumbbell presses

It can help you increase muscle mass, improve shoulder strength and stability, increase your range of motion, and improve your posture.

Dead bug

Lying on your back, raising and lowering opposite arms and legs while keeping your abdominal muscles engaged

Side plank

Lie on your side with your legs straight and your feet stacked on top of each other.

Reverse Crunch

Curl knees towards face, lifting hips without raising mid-back. Hold briefly, then lower feet to ground. Repeat 10-12 times.

Sit-up

Sit-ups help to loosen your spine and hip muscles. It makes your back and hip muscles flexible.

Deadlift

The deadlift is a strengthening exercise where a loaded barbell is lifted off the ground from a stabilized, bent over position, knees free to bend.

Single-leg deadlifts

The single-leg deadlift is an exercise characterized by a forward hip-hinge movement while lifting one leg off the ground and extending it backwards.

Bent-over Row

The bent over row is a multi-jointed exercise that recruits several different muscles.

Leg raises

Leg raises with a twist are among the best ab workouts targeting lower abs, obliques, and hip flexors.

Dancing

Dance, the movement of the body in a rhythmic way, usually to music and within a given space, for the purpose of expressing an idea or emotion
